Why Pipeline Needed?

NLP tools are welcomed in the workflow of text processing, for exmaple, Named Entity Recognition for Chemistry needs workflow of splitter, pos tagger for annotated text.

If we have group of such documents, them a pipeline workflow will be much convenient for a NLP processing task.
